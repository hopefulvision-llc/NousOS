### Coherence Score (0-100%)

**Definition**: Quality of heart rhythm, not just variability
- **High coherence (>75%)**: Smooth, sine-wave-like oscillation â†’ agents active
- **Low coherence (<50%)**: Erratic, chaotic rhythm â†’ agents dormant

**Calculation**: 
- 60% base state coherence
- 25% rhythm regularity (low standard deviation)
- 15% smoothness (consecutive beats don't spike)

### HRV Metrics (Standard Clinical)

- **SDNN**: Standard Deviation of NN intervals (R-R variability)
- **RMSSD**: Root Mean Square of Successive Differences (beat-to-beat changes)
- **Mean R-R**: Average time between heartbeats (milliseconds)

### Agent Activation Analysis

- **Threshold**: 75% coherence
- **Percentage Active**: Time spent above threshold during session
- **Activation Quality**: EXCELLENT / GOOD / MODERATE / LOW / POOR
- **Would Activate**: Boolean for average coherence check
